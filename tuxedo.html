Tuxedo Cats: Fun Facts

A Tuxedo Cat refers not to a breed but rather a color pattern. These stunning bicolor cats resemble someone wearing a tuxedo, thus the name. Tuxedo cats are known to be friendly, lap loving, soft-hearted, lazy, moody, friendly wanderers. They are also rumored to be the smartest cat. Some other fun facts:


  • In recognition of their formal attire, tuxedo cats are the only felines admitted to perfomances of the metropolitan opera.
  • A tuxedo cat earned an Olympic silver medal in the 1960 winter Olympics as a member of the Swiss four man (three man and one cat) bobsled team.
  • A tuxedo cat named Roderick is the only feline ever to climb Mt.Everest!
